Stat19COVID is a statistics visualization website for the numbers and figures of COVID-19 pandemic in Indian states and Districts. World map shown above gives an overview of all the countries across the glove that are affected by the deadly disease. This is a fun activity by college undergrads and by no means intends to reap any commercial benefits.
